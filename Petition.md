# give me data protection

## [mendatory glaze of the system as per the law]

## issues
our laws are outdated and does not meet the global standards   
users dont have any control over their data when it comes to deletion or acess  
increase in scams and identity theft due to lack of a data protection law  
cyber crime law is not enough we also need a data protection law to ensure those handling our data is handling it in a secure manner  
securoty issues has been so bad in this nation to the point where we cant ignore it anymore  
with the advancement of AI the lack of a law for data protection will make the issue even worse  
we cant go forward with a maldives 2.0 without a data protection law in place  
if we cant protect our data within the nation how can we ensure its safe internationally?  
data access issues (some places(police) providing data in outdated formats like CD)  

## solutions
a GDPR style law for data protection in maldives  
mark any data that can be used to identify a person as protected data  
establish a data protection authority to work alongside with cyber sec   
provide data in a easy to access way (usb, email, cloud link)  
harsh fines for both public and private companies who mishandle user data
